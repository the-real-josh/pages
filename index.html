Favourite projects

CFD Solver

  • Made in C++ and Python
  • My most complex and involved coding project that I have ever made
  • Solves for the transonic flow of air through a converging-diverging duct using inviscid Euler equations with RK45 time-stepping and JST dissipation
  • The full project can be viewed at: https://github.com/the-real-josh/cfd-solver-2

Toothless

  • My scratch-built level 1 high-power rocket
  • Named after Toothless from How to Train Your Dragon because of its matte black appearance
  • Experimented with various manufacturing techniques, including forged composites, overwrapped plastic composites, vacuum bagging composites, 3D printed tube mandrels, and manual lathe.
  • Finishing this project involved lots of failures, but resulted in lots of learning.
  • Earned me the certification on its third launch attempt, reaching an apogee of about 4000 ft.

Morpheus

  • Built with SRT
  • High-powered solid rocket designed to hit 30,000 ft
  • Built most of the aft section, rebuilt the nonstructural payload fairing, sanded and painted the vehicle
  • Failed during its final 30,000 ft shot
  • Failure was likely due to a lack of stiffness in the structural payload section.
  • Learned the importance of doing static and transient structures calculations, no matter how "overbuilt" the structure might seem
